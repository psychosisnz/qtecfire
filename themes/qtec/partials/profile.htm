Company Profile


Qtec Fire Services New Zealand is the New Zealand owned distributor for Qtec Fire Suppression Systems made in Australia.

We stock parts for all the major brands in our Christchurch and Palmerston North warehouses. We have four full time staff and five contractors all fully trained and certified for fire suppression systems. Our full time staff complete all service work and most installs.

We cover the whole country based from Christchurch, Palmerston North and Tauranga in our fully mine site compliant vehicles. Qtec Fire Services NZ became a standalone fire service company 5 years ago to provide a comprehensive service package to its customers.

Scott Pilkington, the owner/operator of Qtec Fire Services NZ has been involved in the mining industry for over 20 years contracting in the mining industry. During this time he has serviced mine sites for hydraulics, auto lubrication, extinguishers and fire suppression. He is qualified to design, install and service most types of fire suppression including Dry Powder, Gas Flood, Aerosol and Foam. He also holds NZQA accreditation for hand held firefighting equipment.
